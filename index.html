What ASM Navigator does

From registration to positive payment adjustment

Every ASM requirement covered in one platform. Deploys in two weeks. No IT team required.

Participant Hub
NPI verification, CMS contact form workflow, portal registration tracker, and deadline calendar. Every step guided end-to-end.
Quality Command Center
Tracks all 5 Heart Failure measures in real time. Alerts when data completeness drops below the 75% CMS threshold. Projects your score vs. regional cohort.
Cost Intelligence
Episode-based cost analytics, LACE Index readmission risk flagging, and peer benchmarking. Know your cost position before CMS calculates it.
Care Coordination Suite
CCA templates, PCP partner matching, 90-day attestation tracking, and HRSN screening. Both Improvement Activities managed automatically.
Interoperability Manager
CEHRT ID capture, e-prescribing tracking, HIE attestations, and TIN-level PI score. Eliminates the risk of the -10 point interoperability penalty.
Payment Simulator
Live final score translated into a dollar adjustment on your Part B revenue. See the financial impact of every decision before you make it.
